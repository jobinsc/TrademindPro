Method and controls

The frozen rule was a 10-bar compression breakout with +30 target and −30 stop. Signals used completed candles only, entries occurred at the next one-minute open, same-candle target/stop cases were stop-first, no new signal was allowed after 2:30 PM, and positions were forced flat at 3:13 PM. The result remained negative under 3-, 5- and 7-session rollover rules.

*Partial calendar month. Costs include historical lot size, brokerage, date-effective STT, exchange fees, SEBI charges, stamp duty, GST and one point slippage per side.
