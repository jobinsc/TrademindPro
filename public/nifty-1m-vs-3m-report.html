Decision

Use completed 3-minute candles to understand the market direction and structure. Use 1-minute candles to confirm the entry. Use live Nifty and ATM option quotes for execution. Do not trade from the 3-minute chart alone.

Important limitation

The 3-minute chart looks cleaner, but it hides the order of the three internal 1-minute candles. On volatile days it can merge movements, remove movements or change their measured size.

Recommended next workflow

  1. Completed 3-minute candles: determine bullish, bearish or two-sided market story.
  2. Completed 1-minute candles: confirm breakout, reclaim, rejection or pullback entry.
  3. Live Nifty and ATM option quotes: verify spread and whether 5–7 option points remain capturable.
  4. No new entries after 2:30 PM. Stop analysis and force flat at 3:14 PM.
